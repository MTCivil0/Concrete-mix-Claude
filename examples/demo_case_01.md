# Demo Case 01 — Pavement in South Dakota (F2, PCC 5%)

## Scenario
Bridge approach slab in a freeze-thaw exposed environment.
Aggregate from local quarry; PCC from Western Sugar Cooperative at 5%.

## Inputs
- f'c = 4500 psi
- Exposure: F2, W1
- Max aggregate: 3/4"
- Slump: 1-2"
- FM = 2.77, SG_CA = 2.65, SG_FA = 2.68
- Rodded density CA = 100 lbs/ft³
- Aggregate shape: Gravel with crushed faces
- PCC = 5%, all others = 0%
- Air entrained: Yes

## Expected Results
- w/cm ≤ 0.45 (F2 limit)
- Air content: 6.0%
- PCC flag: optimal range, packing strength gain expected
- QC: ASTM C143, C231, C39 at 7 and 28 days
